Network Infrastructure Survey for Kalola Coffeeshop Perumnas Yogyakarta

13 Jan 2025
By Rosyid Majid

Project Overview

Kalola Coffeeshop Perumnas Yogyakarta required a network infrastructure survey to prepare for a new internet and internal network installation.

The survey focused on evaluating cable routes, identifying required networking equipment, and determining optimal device placement to ensure stable connectivity and efficient network operations.

Survey Objectives

  • Cable Routing Survey: Assess potential cable paths for UTP and power installation.
  • Device Requirement Survey: Identify networking equipment needed based on coverage and capacity.
  • Device Placement Survey: Determine optimal placement for router, switches, and access points.

Responsibilities

  • Stakeholder Coordination: Coordinated with the Person in Charge (PIC) regarding cable routes, device placement, and technical requirements.
  • Site Assessment: Performed on-site evaluation of room layout, power availability, and network expansion potential.
  • Technical Recommendation: Provided hardware and placement recommendations based on survey findings.

Survey Results

Recommended Network Equipment

  • Router: MikroTik RB2011UiAS-RM
  • Access Points: 3 × UniFi AC Lite + PoE Adapter
  • Switch: D-Link DGS-108GL
  • RJ45 Connectors: 25 pcs
  • Network Cabinet: Box Panel size 50 × 60 cm
  • UTP Cable: Commscope Cat 5e
  • Power Extension: 6-outlet power strip + stacker
  • Power Cable: 2 meters

Device Placement Plan

  • Server / Router Location: Back Office (Cashier Support Room)
  • Access Point – Front Area: Customer seating (Front Area)
  • Access Point – Middle Area: Central seating area
  • Access Point – Back Area: Rear customer / staff area

Network Analysis

  • Coverage Optimization: Three access points ensure full Wi-Fi coverage across all customer and operational areas.
  • Scalability: The selected MikroTik router and switch support future bandwidth and device expansion.
  • Structured Cabling: Planned cable routes minimize interference and simplify maintenance.

Outcome

  • Completed a comprehensive network infrastructure survey for Kalola Coffeeshop.
  • Delivered a clear equipment list and placement plan for installation readiness.
  • Enabled efficient implementation with minimized rework during deployment.
  • Demonstrated expertise in pre-installation planning and customer coordination.
